Slabside Revival

This web page is to document my attempt to convert a basket-case of a motorcycle I bought over 10 years ago, into a decent running bike. The bike is a Suzuki GSXR 1100 from 1986, one of the fastest bikes of it's time and an icon for teenage boys :-)

This site is not a How-To in any way, but rather my photos, notes, etc. compiled and made public for your to dig through, laugh at and hopefully send me a comment.
